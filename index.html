Short bio

Camila Acácio de Paiva is a Doctorate student at the Computing Institute of Universidade Federal Fluminense (UFF), currently holding a CNPq Scholarship. She holds a M.S. (2018) degree in Computer Science (Software Engineering) from Universidade Federal de Juiz de Fora (UFJF), and a B.S. (2015) degree in Information Systems from IF Sudeste MG. She held a CAPES scholarship during his Master’s. Her research interests include software testing and databases and provenance.
